About Wavestorm Invitational

The beautiful city of Newport Beach, California will be the host city for the Wavestorm Invitational!

Explore the future of Wavestorming with a lineup of the best Wavestorm jockeys. Discover new techniques to advance your foam game as a Wavestormer.

Honorary Surfers

Our expert surfboarder lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before regerstering for this event!

Want to meet the international Wavestorm community and share skills with some of the world's top foamers, kooks, and rippers? Be the first to know what to expect for the future of wavestorming.

Wavestorm Invitational is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for all Wavestorm cowboys. We look forward to an intensive day of ripping and shenanigans.
